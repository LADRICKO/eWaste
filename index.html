About Us

A Circular Economy Solution

A.M. Odjuvwu Concepts Limited specializes in electronic waste collection, precious metal recovery, refining and sustainable jewelry production.


Our mission is to reduce environmental pollution while recovering valuable resources such as gold, silver and copper from discarded electronics.

Our Services

E-Waste Collection

Collection of computers, mobile phones, PCBs, CPUs, telecommunication equipment and obsolete electronics.

Precious Metal Recovery

Recovery of gold, silver, copper and other valuable metals from electronic waste streams.

Gold Refining

Refining recovered precious metals to high purity levels for commercial and industrial use.

Sustainable Jewelry

Crafting environmentally responsible jewelry from recovered gold and precious metals.
